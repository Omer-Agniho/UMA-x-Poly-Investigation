The Polymarket Gamma metadata for the market slug identifies the market title, market ID, conditionId, questionID, outcomes, and CLOB token IDs. The outcome order maps the first listed CLOB token ID to the YES outcome and the second listed CLOB token ID to the NO outcome, subject to the raw metadata values preserved in MARKET-001. The questionID matches the Polygon child ancillaryDataHash identified inside the Ethereum UMA VotingV2 ancillary wrapper. This connects the public Polymarket market record to the Polygon adapter request and then to the Ethereum UMA round 10310 VoteRevealed events.
